# OPSQAI public roadmap

## v1.0 — GA (2026-07-11) — shipped

- Licensing (Installation + Module), Ed25519, versioned, offline-capable.
- Self-hosted Docker reference, resumable Setup Wizard, Doctor.
- Seven-scenario Disaster Recovery.
- Customer Portal.
- Deployment-mode gate.
- Documentation suite v1.0.

## v1.1 — Q4 2026

- Helm chart as first-class deployment alternative.
- WebAuthn + TOTP MFA baseline.
- More AI adapters: Anthropic, Mistral hosted.
- SIEM-native integrations (Splunk HEC, Elastic).

## v1.2 — Q1 2027

- Multi-region Management Center.
- i18n baseline (DE, RO in addition to EN).
- Marketplace / Partner hooks (currently reserved).

Dates are indicative; priorities shift with customer feedback.
