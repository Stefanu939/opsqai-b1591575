## Architecture Decisions

- **AD-014: Provider is customer-chosen per install.** Alternative (single-vendor lock-in on OpenAI) rejected: incompatible with regulated buyers and sovereign-cloud requirements.
- **AD-015: Embedding dimension is pinned per install.** Alternative (dim per corpus) rejected: complicates cross-corpus retrieval and reindex logic.
